To my forest girl ♥....

Poems ♥

Words to show my love:

She treads on needles beneath the canopy pines
To clear and calm her heart and mind
No fear in sight just strength and might
My Forest girl, she sheds all light

That's where our souls meet, in the dead of night
Dancing around, spirits in flight
They talk a language unknown to all
As they fall on the forest floor

They ooze together all in all
Enwrapped by the needles on the canopy floor
And in no time our souls become one
She's your Forest Girl the pines humm...
