To my forest girl ♥....

Poems ♥

Words to show my love:

In a town where artistry thrives and gleams,
Lilium, a girl, as vibrant as dreams.
With bunnies named Mallard and Gordon in tow,
She treads on a path where adventures grow.

Lilium's heart, an explorer's delight,
Her spirit soars like a kite in flight.
In meadows of canvas and colors so grand,
She roams with her bunnies, hand in hand.

Through galleries echoing with whispers of art,
Lilium weaves dreams with each beat of her heart.
In strokes of emotion, she finds her own way,
Painting her world in the light of the day.
While holding her love his hand, the love will never stray

Mallard and Gordon, her trusty companions,
Bound by a love that never lessens.
They chase the sun's rays, they follow the streams,
In the world of creation, they're a part of her dreams.

With paint-stained hands and adventurous might,
Lilium's world is a canvas of pure delight.
A girl of the gallery, an explorer so free,
In the tapestry of life, a masterpiece she'll always be.
